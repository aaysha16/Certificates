## Skills Covered

The qualifications and experiences showcased in this portfolio cover several critical competencies:

### Clinical & Laboratory Science
* **Good Clinical Practice (GCP)**: Designing, conducting, and reporting human clinical trials in compliance with ethical and regulatory frameworks.
* **Chromatographic Separation (HPLC)**: Preparing samples, managing mobile phases, and running high-performance liquid chromatography columns for pharmaceutical quality control and assay validation.
* **Laboratory Safety & QA**: Adherence to standard operating procedures (SOPs) and safety compliance protocols.

### Computational Biology & Pharmacology
* **Molecular Docking**: Docking small molecular ligands into protein structures to study receptor-ligand interactions and binding affinity using state-of-the-art computational biology suites.
* **Virtual Screening**: Filtering chemical databases to identify potential lead candidates.
* **Ethnopharmacology & Traditional Medicine**: Analyzing and standardizing natural compounds, bioactive extracts, and dietary formulations (such as Unani Aahar).

### Professional Communication & Dissemination
* **Poster Presentation**: Constructing and presenting research findings at international scientific forums (SECON 2025).
* **Scientific & Article Writing**: Reviewing, summarizing, and presenting literature on pharmacological mechanisms and dietary formulations.

### Leadership & Event Logistics
* **Event Coordination**: Managing registration, speakers, and venue logistics for large international academic conventions (SECON 2025) and technical hackathons (Snow Frost Hackathon).
* **Community Engagement**: Volunteering for local awareness campaigns (World Pharmacists Day) and environmental restoration drives (Miyawaki Forest Plantation).
